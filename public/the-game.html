What is The Game?

The Game is pretty simple, it's a mind game with a few rules

  • The goal is to not think about The Game
  • If you remember The Game, you lose
  • When you lose, you have to announce "I lost The Game" out loud (or whatever other way is convenient)
  • You can't lose again for 30 minutes after remembering The Game

PS. You're now playing, welcome to The Game.
